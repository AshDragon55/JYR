About Us

J&Y Refrigeration specializes in the installation and repair of essential home appliances, including air conditioners, refrigerators, washing machines, and stoves. Founded in 2000 by Wardlow Hackett and Louvin Bradshaw, our company has built a reputation for high-quality service and reliability. Wardlow and Louvin are the only two people currently working at J&Y Refrigeration, ensuring each job is handled with care and professionalism. We are committed to delivering fast, reliable, and efficient services to keep your appliances running smoothly.
